Thierry H. Brutman

Research in Finance, Artificial Intelligence & Strategic Governance

Founder and CEO of EDDA Stock Invest Corp. (Chicago, USA) and founder of the EDDA Stock Finance Research Laboratory (France). For over twenty years, Thierry Brutman has developed pioneering frameworks combining forecasting, option theory, and cognitive AI systems.

His research contributions include the Brutman Flexibility Index (BFI), the Maximum Rational Investment (MRI), and the Merchant Tracker. These models have been presented at ICFT, CTIS, AICCS, and published in Wilmott Magazine. He has also been invited as an official speaker at the United Nations CICPPP Committee (Geneva, 2025).

This site gives access to his publications, academic trajectory, and ongoing work on the Volvas Brain, an operational AI-as-Middleware system integrating Forecast Intelligence with enterprise governance.
